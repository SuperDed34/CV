Classical Music Ensemble in Cardiff, South Wales

CAMENAE DE CYMRU

Welcome to Camenae de Cymru, a musical ensemble deeply rooted in the heart of Cardiff, South Wales. Established in 2020, our ensemble specializes in the enchanting melodies of the Baroque and Romantic periods, creating an unforgettable musical experience.

Our Repertoire: We invite you to explore a rich tapestry of sacred and secular songs and music, meticulously curated from the Baroque and Romantic eras to the present day.

Talented Musicians: Our ensemble boasts a roster of exceptionally talented musicians and accompanists, each with their own impressive performance backgrounds. They will not only grace you with ensemble performances but also captivate your heart with solo renditions.
